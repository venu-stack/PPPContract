Public-Private Partnership Contract

Welcome to the Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Contract platform. This platform facilitates collaboration between the government, contractors, users, and regulators for the successful execution of PPP projects.

Through this platform, the government can initiate and manage PPP contracts, assign contractors, regulate project progress, and release funds. Contractors can submit work progress, and users can review and rate the work. The regulator ensures compliance and approves the release of funds.
